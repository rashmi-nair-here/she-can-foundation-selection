For every her

She Can Foundation empowers women through education, advocacy, resources, and community-driven change.

ROOTS

SINCE 2018

She Can Foundation is a non-governmental organization registered under the Indian Society Act, 1860. It is founded and headed by Reeta Mishra (Founder & President). The organization was born out of the understanding that large scale systemic inequalities can only be addressed through intervention at the grassroots level. From grassroots efforts in women’s education, health and skill-building, the foundation has expanded its scope to foster a more equitable and supportive environment for women across the globe.

HOW WE WORK

Grassroots Collaboration

Our operational philosophy is entirely collaborative from the ground up. We work with local communities, civic leaders, and government agencies to develop and implement programs that are well-suited to specific regional contexts. By listening directly to the people on the ground, we ensure that our interventions are responding to the real, lived challenges of the women and girls we serve.

Public Trust

Everything we accomplish is based on the foundation of public trust. We grow our operations safely and effectively through the complete transparency we maintain with our network of passionate individuals, corporate CSR initiatives and institutional partners. Together, this financial and strategic support enables us to extend our reach and amplify our impact each and every day.

VISION, MISSION, VALUES

VISION

To transform society and create a beautifully equitable world where every woman has the unfettered opportunity to thrive, succeed and live in dignity.

MISSION

To enable the underprivileged women and girls of the world with affordable education, basic health care assistance, complete skill training and strong employment opportunities.

VALUES

Empowerment: Developing self-reliance and independence.
Equity: Removing social and economic barriers to level the playing field.
Collaboration: We believe that a sustainable social revolution can only be achieved through unified, collective action.
Accountability: Bring powerful results to the community with transparency.
