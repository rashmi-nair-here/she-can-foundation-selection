WHY HER?

If half the population is being held back by systemic barriers, then we can’t effectively move forward as a society. Millions of disadvantaged women and young girls across the globe face serious challenges due to a lack of basic resources, limited access to education and poor healthcare, such as missing school just because they don’t have menstrual hygiene support.

Breaking down these walls is not just a women’s issue — it is a global must. When we empower a woman, we transform a family, uplift a community and change the future of a whole society. Every woman deserves the right to stand tall, thrive and succeed. We are focused entirely on the individual – the young girl working to stay in school, the woman working for financial independence and the mother working for a healthier life. We exist to ensure she has a voice, equal opportunities, reliable support system and the dignity she naturally deserves to build a self-reliant life.

FOR HER, WITH HER

To train women with vocational and professional skills to enable sustainable employment.

Essential Resources

Giving important health and personal care items like sanitary and hygiene kits to young girls.

Education & Support

Providing education programs and giving immediate relief and support in times of crisis.

Advocacy

Spearheading localised awareness campaigns to challenge societal barriers and change attitudes around women’s rights.

She Can Foundation has created a sustainable network of change through a potent mix of localised action and global vision. Working closely with local communities, the foundation has been able to keep young girls in classrooms by providing consistent menstrual hygiene kits to reduce school absenteeism through grassroots organisations and government bodies. What’s more, they have brought women into the workforce through dedicated skill-building workshops and created broad public awareness on key health and social issues through strategic advocacy campaigns.
